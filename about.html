About Air India

Founded in 1932, Air India is India's leading airline, connecting millions of passengers across the globe. As a pioneer in aviation, we take pride in offering world-class service, state-of-the-art aircraft, and a commitment to safety and reliability. Our mission is to provide seamless travel experiences, ensuring comfort, efficiency, and the best customer service in the industry.

With an extensive fleet of modern aircraft, including the Boeing 787 Dreamliner and Airbus A320neo, Air India serves over 100 destinations worldwide. We focus on innovation, sustainability, and excellence, making us the preferred choice for travelers seeking luxury and affordability.

Our dedicated crew and ground staff work tirelessly to provide exceptional service. From priority boarding to exclusive in-flight entertainment, we ensure a smooth and enjoyable journey. Join us in experiencing the best of Indian hospitality at 35,000 feet.
